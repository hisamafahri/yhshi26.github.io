About

I'm Yosra Hashim, a direct-entry medical student studying abroad from Canada. I'm currently in year 2/6 for my program which means we're covering clinical aspects through lectures and dissections.

The time I spent in the lab this year (though limited) definitely stood out to me. It was my first time around donated cadavers and surgical tools. I'll probably write more about the experience in a post, but it's safe to say that the time I spent in the lab had a profound impact on me, and I'm more interested than ever in potentially pursuing a surgical career.
